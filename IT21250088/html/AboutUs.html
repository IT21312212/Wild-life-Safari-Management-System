About Us

We are Sri Lanka’s Safari and Wildlife specialists! But that’s not all; we are also one of the finest nature related tour operators in Sri Lanka having pioneered innovative safari and wildlife tours that have changed the wildlife-expedition landscape in Sri Lanka.Most importantly we are Sri Lankan Safari nature enthusiasts who are passionate about nature and wildlife, not surprising; Every safari tour, expedition and holiday is based on our own first hand experiences, all time tested and authentic.

Believe us, we are truly mad about the wilds of Sri Lanka! This passion for the wild has taken us to every corner which the popular wildlife destinations and the less frequented and unspoiled spots packed with rich biological diversity. All this experience has helped us to come up with some of the most authentic, unique and fulfilling safari expeditions ever.

We have over years of experience in the Sri Lankan Hospitality industry has enhanced us with some of the best resource personnel, vast knowledge and experience, best accommodation options and our own fleet of vehicles, enabling us to give clients real value for money. Our Wildlife Safaris and Bird Watching tours have been acclaimed as some of the finest by nature and wildlife enthusiasts coming from all over the world. Our own safari trackers and naturalists will help customize the safari offering to suit client’s requirements; there’s no rush, no hassle, it’s your vacation so enjoy it just the way you want to do it.

We invites you to join them in discovering some amazing wildlife in unforgettable Sri Lanka.
